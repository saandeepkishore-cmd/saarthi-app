Saarthi

"Your conversations, understood."

Saarthi means "guide" or "charioteer" — someone who helps you navigate, never who steers for you. That's the idea behind every AI feature here: quiet, opt-in help that makes conversations clearer, safer, and a little warmer, without ever taking the wheel.

Saarthi syncs real accounts and messages between everyone using this app through a shared Firebase database, and calls, transcripts, and text AI features run through a shared server-side proxy so no one needs their own API key. There's no OTP/identity verification, so treat contact identities the way you would in any app without phone verification. AI features that read personal content are opt-in and can be switched off individually in Settings.
